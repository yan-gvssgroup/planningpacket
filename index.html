Individual KPIs

Led by George
Already started — draft themes from the July 11 call
01Ownership & Follow-Through
Not just doing what was asked — owning whether the solution actually solves the problem, and saying so if it doesn't.
02Reduction of Founder Dependency
Fewer decisions coming back to George. The team runs the full arc after his initial sign-off, without needing him mid-project.
03Milestone & Project Completion
Dates get hit once "done" is clearly defined up front — needs a shared definition of done per project.
04Business & Operational Outcomes
Tied back to something countable where possible — dollars saved, revenue added, time reclaimed.
05Timeliness & Reliability
Work lands on time and at the expected quality level, consistently, not just occasionally.
06Solutions Orientation
Bringing a recommendation alongside a problem, not just flagging it and waiting on George to decide.

These were named live, not as a finished KPI list but as raw material — genuinely hard to pin down before projects are even planned. Which of the six can become an actual number before Day 2, and which stay qualitative for this half?

Give the destination, not the driving directions — delegate the outcome, not the how. Do the themes above measure outcomes, or do a few of them still quietly measure process?
